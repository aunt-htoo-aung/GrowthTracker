\# DevTrack - Personal Progress Tracking System



\## Project Overview



DevTrack is a web-based personal progress tracking system designed to help learners, developers, and professionals document their daily learning activities and accomplishments.



The system allows users to create daily progress entries, organize them using dynamic tags, and visualize their consistency through a GitHub-style contribution calendar. By transforming daily efforts into measurable progress, DevTrack promotes continuous learning, self-reflection, and accountability.



---



\## Problem Statement



Many learners and developers struggle to keep track of what they learn and accomplish each day. Although platforms like GitHub provide contribution graphs for coding activities, they do not capture other forms of learning such as reading technical books, studying new concepts, completing courses, practicing skills, or working on personal projects.



As a result, users often lose visibility into their growth and find it difficult to maintain consistent learning habits.



---



\## Proposed Solution



DevTrack provides a centralized platform where users can:



\- Record daily learning activities and achievements

\- Organize entries using dynamic tags

\- Search and review historical records

\- Track consistency through contribution calendars

\- Analyze learning trends and growth over time

\- Share articals and blob



The platform acts as both a personal journal and a progress tracking system.



---
